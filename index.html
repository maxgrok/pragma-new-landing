About Us

Pragma was born June 2019 and will launch a private beta in July 2019 for Baltimore.

In 2011, living in a church basement, Max started putting together maps of free and low-cost resources local to their area. This was how they and their friends knew where to get help, fast, and legally in 2012. Fast forward, Max has become a software engineer with the ability to build bigger, better maps with reviews.
